YUVDroidTools
=========
A tool for YUV raw data preview on Android platform.

Features
=========
- Support a variety of YUV format, such as I420, NV12, YUV422P etc.
- Support a variety of sws convert algorithm, such as FAST_BILINEAR, BILINEAR, BICUBIC etc.
